Technical Analysis - Bullish

Buy Rules (BTO)

  • Short and Medium Term Uptrend

    Medium: 50SMA above 150SMA. Both sloping up.
    Short: 20EMA above 40EMA.

  • At least 2 out of 3 Bullish Indicators

    Parabolic SAR
    Force Index
    MACD (Black Above Red)

  • No Strong Resistance

    Less than 4 ATR away.

  • Dip on Uptrend / Breakout / Reversal Pattern

    Price is near 50/150/200SMA.
    Tested Resistance.

Sell Rules (STC)

  • Cut Losses IMPORTANT

    1 ATR below Key Support Line / Moving Average

  • Stock Goes Into Downtrend

    20 Day EMA below 40 Day EMA

  • Protecting Profits

    Trailing Stop Loss 2 ATR from the high
    (When price extends far beyond the MA)

    When it hits a profit of 2R and one of the 3 indicators turn bearish

    MACD(black) below signal(red)

    When the price hits a pre-determined profit target (1.5R, 2R, etc)
