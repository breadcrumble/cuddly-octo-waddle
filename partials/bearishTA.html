Technical Analysis - Bearish

FinViz Bearish Screener

Fundamental - Sell Rules (STO) (Any will do)

  • Overvalued
  • Hit by Very Bad News

Buy Rules (BTO)

  • Short and Medium Term Downtrend

    Medium: 50SMA below 150SMA. Both sloping down.
    Short: 20EMA below 40EMA.

  • At least 2 out of 3 Bearish Indicators

    Parabolic SAR
    Force Index
    MACD (Red Above Black)

  • No Strong Support

    Less than 4 ATR away.

  • Price is near 50/150/200SMA.

    Is a proven resistance for that stock.

  • Breakdown from Consolidation / Rally on DownTrend / Reversal Pattern

    See Below.

Breakdown from Consolidation

Suport Tested at least 3 times.
Narrower and longer Consolidations usually mean stronger breakdowns.
Candle Body Opens and Closes Below the Support

Buy Point 1: First Breakout
Buy Point 2: After it retraced and tested the support (now resistance).

SELL Stop Limit order 3 cents below the LOW (Bullish) / Close (Bearish) of the Breakout Candle.
BUY Stop Loss 1 ATR above support.

Rally on DownTrend

Close to a (Proven) Support MA.
OR Breaking a Counter Trend Line Resistance
When the chart makes a long rally on the downtrend, draw a support line.
When the full body of a candle breaks the support, it's a SELL point.
Stop Loss 1 ATR above support.

Reversal

50SMA cross below 150SMA
2 out of 3 Momentum indicators are bearish.
Stop Loss 1 ATR above Support.

Buy to Cover Rules (BTC)

  • Cut Losses IMPORTANT

    1 ATR above Key Resistance Line / Moving Average

  • Stock Goes Into Uptrend

    20 Day EMA crosses above 40 Day EMA

  • Protecting Profits

    1. Trailing Stop Loss 2 ATR from the low
    (When price extends far beyond the MA)

    2. When it hits a profit of 2R and one of the 3 indicators turn bullish
    e.g. MACD(black) below signal(red)

    When the price hits a pre-determined profit target (1.5R, 2R, etc)
