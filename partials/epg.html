Earnings Probability Game Short Term, Bullish

Expected Results: 15-30% per Quarter


Screening Criteria

  • 1. Zacks Screenings

    ESP (Earnings Surprise Prediction) > 3%
    Zacks Rank 1 or 2
    Average Volume above 100,000 shares
    Positive surprise in the last quarter

  • 2. Sort By Date (Which will come first?)

    Take note of the time when the earnings will be released.
    (Before Market opens or After Market Closes?)

  • 3. Stock Is On Uptrend

    Stock is on Clear Uptrend

Opening and Closing Positions

  • Opening Positions

    Place orders one day before earnings are released!

    BUY MOC (market on close) + Bracket Order (See Below)
    STOP LOSS order (Cut Loss): 1 ATR below purchase price
    (*Average ATR over past 6 months. NOT highest ATR.)
    SELL LIMIT ORDER (Take Profit): 2 ATR above the purchase price

  • Closing Position

    Wait for Price to Hit Either Stop Loss or Profit target within 2 Days

    OPTIONAL: If the price moves 1R In the Money, Raise Stop Loss to Breakeven Point.

    IMPORTANT: In 2 days, if the Price does not hit Profit Target or Stop Loss, Exit Manually.
