Bounce Strategy Short Term

First do Fundamental Analysis for Steps 2 and 3. Just treat this as another entry point.


Entry Criteria

  • Fundamental Analysis

    Must satisfy Steps 2 and 3 of WA 10 Steps.

  • Candle Reversal Pattern

    1. Body is above Support
    2. Tail cuts through previous low (tail)
    3. Tail cuts through MA support

  • Moving Averages

    EMA: 20, 40 | SMA: 50, 100, 150, 200
    All MAs must be intact (Shorter Term above Longer Term), and sloping up.
    Candle reversal pattern ONLY take place on the 50, 100, 150, 200 SMA.

  • Full Stochastic Indicator

    Full Stochastics (5, 3, 3) | Period: 5, %K: 3, %D: 3
    BOTH %K and % D lines are BELOW the 30 level
    BONUS: %K line crosses above the %D line

Opening and Closing Positions

  • Opening Positions

    BUY STOP ORDER: A few cents above trigger candle.
    STOP LOSS SELL order (Cut Loss): A few cents below trigger candle.
    SELL LIMIT ORDER (Take Profit):2.5R above buy order distance

    NOTE: Purchase Price to Stop Loss (AKA 1R) must be at least 1*ATR.

  • Closing Position

    Usually Position will close by itself.
    But the body of subsequent candles cuts below the MA, it becomes invalid. Just close the position!
