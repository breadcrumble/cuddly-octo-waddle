Technical Analysis - Bullish

FinViz Bullish Screener

Buy Rules (BTO)

  • Short and Medium Term Uptrend

    Medium: 50SMA above 150SMA. Both sloping up.
    Short: 20EMA above 40EMA.

  • At least 2 out of 3 Bullish Indicators

    Parabolic SAR
    Force Index
    MACD (Black Above Red)

  • No Strong Resistance

    Less than 4 ATR away.

  • Price is near 50/150/200SMA.

    Is a proven support for that stock.

  • Breakout / Dip on Uptrend / Reversal Pattern

    See Below.

Breakout

Resistance Tested at least 3 times.
Narrower and longer Consolidations usually mean stronger breakouts.
Candle Body Opens and Closes Above the Resistance

Buy Point 1: First Breakout
Buy Point 2: After it retraced and tested the resistance.

BUY Stop Limit order 3 cents above the High (Bearish) / Close (Bullish) of the Breakout Candle.
Stop Loss 1 ATR below resistance.

Dip on Uptrend

Close to a (Proven) Support MA.
OR Breaking a Counter Trend Line Resistance
When the chart makes a long dip on the uptrend, draw a resistance line.
When the full body of a candle breaks the resistance, it's a BUY point.
Stop Loss 1 ATR below resistance.

Reversal

50SMA cross over 150SMA
2 out of 3 Momentum indicators are bullish.
Stop Loss 1 ATR below resistance.

Sell Rules (STC)

  • Cut Losses IMPORTANT

    1 ATR below Key Support Line / Moving Average

  • Stock Goes Into Downtrend

    20 Day EMA below 40 Day EMA

  • Protecting Profits

    1. Trailing Stop Loss 2 ATR from the high
    (When price extends far beyond the MA)

    2. When it hits a profit of 2R and one of the 3 indicators turn bearish
    e.g. MACD(black) below signal(red)

    When the price hits a pre-determined profit target (1.5R, 2R, etc)
