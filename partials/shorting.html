EPS Updating Method The Easy Way

After screening and finding stocks which have solid fundamentals for the past 4 Years (Step 2).
As well as good EPS numbers (Step 3), it is important to ensure that those EPS numbers stay healthy as subsequent earnings are released.

Hence, this method.

  • Search on Finviz (FinViz EPS Screener) for Positive EPS Growth This Year, Next Year, Past 5 Years and Next 5 Years.
  • Compare the existing watchlist with the Finviz list
  • If a stock on the watchlist does not appear on the FinViz list, re-examine it or kick it out.
